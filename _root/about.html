Henry Addo - @eyedol

Senior software developer at Ushahidi

A Little About Me

I am a software developer from Accra, Ghana currently living in Sendai, Japan. I have a strong passion for mobile development, web development and open technologies. I have been in this industry for close to 7 years now and I have gained so much experience. I have a proven track record of successful projects. I am capable of working independently and I do this well with a distributed team.

I am currently a senior software developer at Ushahidi, a non-profit technology company that focuses on building tools for data collection, visualization and interactive mapping.

I have an interest in meeting people and I love to connect people with the right resources and the support they need.

Personal

I am a geek and a husband. I have practised the art of juggling between the two.

This year my wife and I moved from Ghana to live in a bike-friendly city of Sendai, Japan. I commute to work and through the city by bicycle. I only use mass transit when it's necessary.

Passion

I have a keen interest in being a good photographer. I finally faced reality and invested in a decent camera to pursue my passion.

I love open technologies and I have been instrumental in the evangelism and advocacy of the use of Free and Open Source Software in Ghana.

I enjoy riding motorcycles. In fact I love anything on two wheels.

Work

At Ushahidi I lead the development efforts of Android apps for it's web based tools and contribute to major parts of the core Ushahidi web platform.

In 2012, I founded FindReels.com, a comprehensive movie guide for Ghanaian cinemas.

In 2007, I was awarded TEDGlobal fellowship which granted me a free pass to attend the prestigious TED conference in Arusha, Tanzania.

I have worked as a software developer with several IT companies including Suuch Solutions, txtGhana and IDZ Ghana Limited. I also worked as a Java instructor at IPMC Ghana, an IT training institution.

Travel

I love to travel. I have been afforded the opportunity to do quite a bit of traveling for both work and personal reasons. This has enabled me to learn so much about the world. I appreciate people along with their various cultures, lagnuages, similarities and differences. You can follow my longitude map on crowdmap to see where I have been.
